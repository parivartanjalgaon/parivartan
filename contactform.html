Testimonial

Parivartan, Jalgaon is a non-governmental organization from Jalgaon (Khandesh), Maharashtra. Shambhu Patil and his friends jointly organize different activities related to the literature, culture, drama, movies and many more things. Though their lot of work is in the Marathi language, they are not limited to. They invite many reputed personalities for the interaction with common people. Their drama 'Apurnak' (Marathi version famous Hindi drama 'Adhe Adhure') got state-level recognition. Since 15th October 2015, every year they were organizing 7-day 'Parivartan-Vachan Prerna Mahotsav' in which they every day read (Abhivachan) some book.
